Frequently Asked Questions

+ What is First Aid?

First aid is the immediate assistance provided to someone who is injured or ill before professional medical help arrives.

+ How to perform CPR?

CPR involves chest compressions and rescue breaths. The current recommended ratio is 30 compressions to 2 breaths for adults.

+ When should I call emergency services?

Call emergency services immediately in cases of severe bleeding, unconsciousness, chest pain, difficulty breathing, or any life-threatening situation.
